Our Ongoing Projects

Food Security

Project Seva

Food distribution to slum areas, orphanages, dry ration kits, no-food-waste campaigns. Volunteers move surplus and support directly to people who need immediate meals.

Impact: 50,000+ meals distributed.

Environment

Project Prakriti

Tree plantation, plastic-free zones, waste segregation. The work turns public spaces into cleaner, greener community habits.

Impact: 20,000+ trees planted.

Education

Project Bachpanshala

Open-air rural education, school supplies for underprivileged children. Learning is brought closer to children whose classrooms are not always formal.

Impact: Rural education support.

Women

Project Udaan

Women empowerment through digital campaigns, skill training, financial independence. The programme builds confidence, capability, and access.

Impact: 900+ girls empowered.

Animal Care

Project Jeev

Street animal feeding, rescue, care. Daily feeding and emergency help bring care to animals on the street.

Impact: 50+ animals fed daily.

Livelihood

Project Vikas

Vocational training for youth and women, employability skills. The project focuses on practical skills that can translate into work.

Impact: Youth and women trained for employability.
